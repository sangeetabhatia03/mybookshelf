A Century of Books - Reading List

In 2014 I took up a reading challenge - read one book for each year of the century. Sadly, I have not yet completed this challenge, but I'll get there. In the meanwhile, this challenge has merged with my other interests. Here I am using D3 to track my progress. The green rectangles indicate the years for which I have read a book published in the year. Hovering over the rectangle shows a tooltip containing the name of the book and the author. The red rectangles are obviously the ones for which I am yet to read a book.

The sidebar will eventually list all the books I have. At the moment though, the list is far from complete.
